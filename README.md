## What Is SwampWater?

SwampWater is a university-focused beverage platform that connects:

- **Universities** — licensing departments, NIL collectives, athletic partnerships
- **Distilleries & Co-Packers** — beverage producers ready for campus distribution
- **Distributors & Retail** — campus-area bars, liquor stores, and licensed venues

We structure licensed, campus-specific beverage partnerships and own the full rollout — from compliance and co-packer coordination to channel launch and reorder.

---

## The Model

```
University  ──►  SwampWater  ──►  Producer / Distillery
                     │
                     ▼
           Campus Bars · Retail · Events
```

1. **University Partnership** — licensing alignment, brand rights, compliance
2. **Producer Matchmaking** — right distillery for the right campus
3. **Launch Architecture** — product identity, NIL, QR activation, ambassador program
4. **Channel Rollout** — bars, retail, licensed venues, reorder cadence
